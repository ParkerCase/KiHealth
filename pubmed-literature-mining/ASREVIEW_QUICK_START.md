## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: Do I have to approve each article?**
A: No! Just label them Relevant or Irrelevant. That's the approval.

**Q: Can I skip articles?**
A: Yes, but not recommended. AI learns from every decision. If unsure, mark as Irrelevant.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: You can go back and change labels later in ASReview.

**Q: How many do I need to screen?**
A: Start with 50-100 to train AI, then screen as many as you want. Minimum 200 recommended.
