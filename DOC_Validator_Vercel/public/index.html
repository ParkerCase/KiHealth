Required CSV Columns:

  • age - Patient age (30-85 years) - Required
  • sex - 1=Male, 0=Female
  • bmi - Body Mass Index (15-50 kg/m²)
  • womac_r - Right knee WOMAC score (0-96)
    OR vas_r - Right knee VAS pain (0-10) - will auto-convert to WOMAC
    *Optional - improves accuracy but not required
  • womac_l - Left knee WOMAC score (0-96)
    OR vas_l - Left knee VAS pain (0-10) - will auto-convert to WOMAC
    *Optional - improves accuracy but not required
  • kl_r - Right knee KL grade (0-4) or "na" for Not available
    Required - at least one knee must have imaging data (KL grade or "na")
    *If both knees have KL grades, at least one must be ≥1
  • kl_l - Left knee KL grade (0-4) or "na" for Not available
    Required - at least one knee must have imaging data (KL grade or "na")
    *If both knees have KL grades, at least one must be ≥1
  • fam_hx - Family history (1=Yes, 0=No) *Optional - defaults to 0 (No) if not provided
  • tkr_outcome - TKR occurred (1=Yes, 0=No) *Optional - for validation metrics

Note: If using VAS scores, they will be automatically converted to WOMAC using the validated formula: WOMAC = (VAS × 8) + 15 (based on Salaffi et al. 2003, correlation r ≈ 0.68-0.72). Estimated uncertainty: ±10-15 WOMAC points.
